It typically refers to royalty-free or generic music that can be used in various projects without copyright issues. What is Music Generics? Music generics encompass a wide range of royalty-free music tracks that can be used for personal or commercial projects without the need for licensing fees. This type of music is ideal for creators looking to enhance their videos, podcasts, presentations, or other media without worrying about copyright infringement.
